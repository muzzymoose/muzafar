The Challenge

Without visibility into cost drivers, insurers struggle to price policies accurately. The company needed to understand what factors influence medical expenses and predict costs for new applicants to set appropriate premiums.

Approach

  • EDA to identify cost patterns across demographics (age, BMI, smoking, region)
  • Feature engineering: BMI categories, age groups, risk scores
  • Trained Gradient Boosting model with k-fold cross-validation
  • Integrated Python predictions directly into Power BI for real-time quotes

Key Findings

  • Smoking is the #1 cost driver — smokers cost 2.8× more ($32K vs $11K avg)
  • Costs escalate exponentially after age 50 (up to $28.5K avg for 60+)
  • Obesity + smoking = highest risk profile at $40K+ annually
  • Quote generation reduced from 45 min to 10 min with the prediction tool
